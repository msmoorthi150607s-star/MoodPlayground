FAQs

What is FeelSync?

FeelSync is an interactive platform by TeamFive that lets you explore different moods through vibrant themes, playful animations, and personalized experiences.

How does the AI voice assistant work?

Our friendly AI voice greets you, asks how you're feeling, and responds with mood-based visuals, sounds, and animations. It even shows a waveform animation while speaking.

Can I personalize my experience?

Yes! You can enter your name to receive personalized greetings, track your moods over time, and enjoy tailored quotes, jokes, and surprises.

Can I use FeelSync on mobile?

Yes, the platform is fully responsive and works smoothly on both desktop and mobile devices.
